This project is the client side of Hiring Company package.
It contains methods for logging in and out, presenting company's employees, partner companies and projects.
Workspaces differ depending on the type of logged-in employee.
CEO can add employees, while product owners can make new projects which CEO can accept or decline.
Then CEO can send project to partner outsourcing company and be notified if the outsourcing company accepted that project.
PO recieves user stories from outsourcing company and decides whether to approve them or not. 
PO is informed when all user stories for certain project are closed and then he can mark that project as closed.
